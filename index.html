About Us

Welcome to the Editorial Board of NMIMS's MPSTME! Our dedicated team of experienced professionals and experts plays a crucial role in shaping the content and direction of our events and the publication of our magazine, Verve. As stewards of quality and integrity, we are committed to upholding the highest standards in journalism, research, and communication.

Our Mission

Our mission is to curate, review, and publish content that informs, educates, and engages our audience. We strive to foster a culture of open dialogue and diverse perspectives while maintaining a commitment to accuracy, relevance, and ethical journalism.
